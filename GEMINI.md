## Build Commands
- Build: `xcodebuild build -scheme SwiftVerificarWCAGAlgs -destination 'platform=macOS'`
- Test: `xcodebuild test -scheme SwiftVerificarWCAGAlgs -destination 'platform=macOS'`
- NEVER use `swift build` or `swift test`
